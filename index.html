About Us

Our Story

Imelda Tria Katigbak opened Nap's Restaurant & Bar in 2017 with the goal of creating a cozy and welcoming environment where patrons could enjoy delectable food and beverages while creating lasting memories with friends and family. Since then, we have developed into a beloved business recognized for our dedication to offering great service and culinary experiences.

Our menu showcases the varied and lively flavors of Filipino cooking, from traditional favorites like adobo and sinigang to creative dishes inspired by local ingredients. Our journey is deeply rooted in Filipino cuisine, blending traditional flavors with modern twists to offer a unique dining experience that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines.

Our Team

Get to know the enthusiastic people who own Nap's Restaurant & Bar. Each member of our team is a committed professional with a different set of skills and knowledge. We put forth endless effort as a team to make sure Nap's Restaurant & Bar provides outstanding dining experiences that go above and beyond for our customers.

Our intention is to establish a warm environment where each visitor is made to feel like a member of our family. We work hard to give everyone who enters our doors first-rate service, mouthwatering cuisine, and treasured memories. Our mission is to be a pillar of the community, building relationships and making treasured moments that will last for years to come. We will achieve this by committing to excellence and having a passion for hospitality.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with the highest standards of integrity and ethics.
  • Innovation: We embrace innovation and continuously seek new ways to improve and evolve.
  • Customer Satisfaction: We prioritize the satisfaction and success of our customers above all else.
